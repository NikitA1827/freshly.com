Common Question

I’m a new customer. How do I redeem my gift card?

Redeem your Gift Card online at freshly.com/gifts. When redeemed and if the order amount is greater than the amount on your gift card, your provided payment method will be charged for the difference. If the gift card balance exceeds the amount of your order, your remaining gift card balance will be converted to stored credit and automatically applied to future orders. If your order amount and gift card have equal value, then the full gift card balance will be used on the order. By using this card, you agree to these Terms & Conditions and Freshly reserves the right to modify, replace, or suspend gift cards at any time. This Gift Card does not expire and is not reloadable. No replacement if lost or stolen. Not valid on prior purchases, refer a friend programs, applicable taxes, shipping fees or redeemable for cash or cash equivalents.

Does the recipient of the gift card need a Freshly subscription to redeem their gift?

No, your gift card’s recipient does not need to have a Freshly subscription in order to redeem. Upon redeeming their gift card, they’ll be prompted to create an account, which will jump start their subscription.

What if my gift card amount is greater than my order total?

If the gift card balance exceeds the amount of your order when redeemed, your remaining gift card balance will be converted to stored credit and automatically applied to future orders. If your order amount is greater than the amount on your gift card, your provided payment method will be charged for the difference. If your order amount and gift card have equal value, then the full gift card balance will be used on the order.

Why is my credit card information needed to redeem my gift card?

Gift card recipients that are new customers, will need to enter their credit card information upon checkout in case their order total exceeds the gift card amount. If the order amount is greater than the amount on your gift card, your provided payment method will be charged for the difference. Additionally, once your first order is placed with your gift card amount, your Freshly subscription is activated and the credit card information you entered will be charged for any and all subsequent orders.

Can I schedule the gift card to be sent on a specific day?

Yes! You’ll have the option to choose a specific date and time that your gift card will be delivered to your recipient.

Do gift card purchases automatically result in the creation of a subscription?

No, gift card purchases do not result in a subscription with us. They are only one-time gift card purchases.

If I redeem a gift card as a new customer, am I starting a subscription with Freshly?

Yes, if a new customer redeems their gift card, they automatically start a subscription with their gift card activation.

I was given a gift card, but haven’t redeemed it yet. Does it expire?

Nope! Gift cards don’t expire and will not decrease in value if not used.
